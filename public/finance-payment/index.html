Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many organizations I can manage?
No! All plans now include unlimited organization wallets, so you can manage as many organizations as you need without restrictions.
How do organization wallets work with member wallets?
Each organization has a master wallet, and each member within the organization has their own sub-wallet. Funds can be allocated and managed between organization and member wallets seamlessly.
Can we process payments across multiple organizations?
Yes, our platform supports bulk payments across multiple organizations with centralized tracking and reporting. You can group organizations and process payments in batches.
What payment gateways are supported?
We support Stripe, Paystack, Flutterwave, PayPal, and other major payment providers. Enterprise plans can integrate custom payment solutions.
