Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create both free and paid events?
Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans support both free and paid ticketing with integrated payment processing and fee management.
How does the QR code check-in work?
Attendees receive a unique QR code via email. Event organizers can scan these codes using our mobile app for instant check-in and attendance tracking.
Can I host webinars and virtual events?
Absolutely! Our platform includes built-in webinar hosting, live streaming, breakout rooms, and recording capabilities for virtual and hybrid events.
Are certificates automatically generated?
Yes, certificates can be automatically generated and sent to attendees based on attendance criteria you set. You can also manually issue certificates.
